## üìê Design Style

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Clean and minimal** - Less is more
- **White background** - Fresh and professional
- **Plenty of white space** - Don't overcrowd
- **Premium and modern** - High-end tech academy feel

### Visual Elements
- **Glassmorphism cards**: Soft blue borders, subtle shadows
- **Subtle tech patterns**: Circuit lines, neural networks (light blue, not overpowering)
- **Icons**: Simple, modern line icons in ocean blue
- **Illustrations**: Clean laptop/tech illustrations, rockets for deployment

### What to AVOID
- ‚ùå Dark/black backgrounds (unless specifically requested)
- ‚ùå Too many elements or text
- ‚ùå Cluttered designs
- ‚ùå Generic stock photos
- ‚ùå Icons with brain graphics around logo
